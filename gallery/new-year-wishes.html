New Year Wishes. 2022


Details - Original Work

  • Size: 16cm x 18.5cm (6.5x7.5")
  • Mediums: colored pencils, metallic ballpoint pen (gold), drawing paper (white)

Story/Comment by the Artist

2021 was a strange year, so I drew this in January 2022, wishing for a New Year full of roses and wonders :)

Later in 2022 I gave this art as a gift to a friend who encouraged me to pursue an art career - new to me!
