Beautiful World. 2022


Details - Original Work

  • Size: A4/ 21cm x 29.7cm (8.27x11.69")
  • Mediums: colored pencils, drawing paper (white)

Story/Comment by the Artist

When I drew this, I was still unsure about what just happened in my life. Both the physical and emotional foundations had just shattered.

Then I realized the many people who immediately showed their support and care for me. These people and such kind acts make the world truly beautiful.
