Jumping into the Unknown. 2021


Details - Original Work

  • Size: A4/ 21cm x 29.7cm (8.27x11.69")
  • Mediums: colored pencils, drawing paper (black)

Story/Comment by the Artist

This is when I quit my secure, familiar job in the US and left the country alone in Feb 2021.

I didn't know what exactly I'd do or where to go next, but something was calling me to take a leap of faith and go with the flow, so I did.
