Autumn Dream. 2021


Details - Original Work

  • Size: A4/ 21cm x 29.7cm (8.27x11.69")
  • Mediums: colored pencils, print paper

Story/Comment by the Artist

This was drawn out of my mind's eye in the summer of 2021.

Having spent most of my life in tropical areas, I daydreamed an immersion in a colorful autumn forest. Interestingly, I got a work assignment in Berlin, Germany later that year, so my wish was fulfilled.
