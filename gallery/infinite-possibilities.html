Infinite Possibilities. 2022


Details - Original Work

  • Size: A4/ 21cm x 29.7cm (8.27x11.69")
  • Mediums: colored pencils, drawing paper (black)

Story/Comment by the Artist

You might have heard that we create our own reality.

A large part of my 2022 has been challenging. My life was shaken to the core, and my beliefs were tested repeatedly. Finally, close to the end of the tunnel, I felt I could be "in charge" of the world I was experiencing. The power is in my hands.
