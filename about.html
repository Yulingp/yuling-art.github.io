About the Artist, Yuling Peng (彭郁舲)

My life is my canvas. I paint dreams!

Before 2021, most people knew me for my "intellectual" work because of my academic and work backgrounds (listed below), but imagination and creativity are the secret sauce for my happiness.

I drew a lot when I was a kid. Each year, I drew or made birthday and holiday cards for my parents. I also drew in my textbooks and test sheets (?!) I published an illustration book in Taiwan in 2011.

I was born in the US, grew up in Taiwan, and have lived in Canada, France, Spain, Germany, and Estonia. Being a traveling soul, I'm usually in between Austin, TX; Tallinn, Estonia; Barcelona, Spain; and Kaohsiung, Taiwan. They're all my homes!

My Creative Process

I like to draw emotions or states of mind based on my real-life experience. When a life event, person, or thing evokes a rather strong feeling, a desire to draw could strike me.

Then I'd close my eyes and imagine how that specific feeling would visually look like. Usually, a visual image would appear in my head. Then I'd try to sketch the vision out on paper. Oftentimes, there's no detail yet. As I continue, I decide on the details, colors and complementary objects on the go.
